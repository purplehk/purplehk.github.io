Picnic at MOS Park

Hosted by: Christine Dormeier

Event Type: Playdate, Outdoor, Potluck

Date: Saturday, April 10, 2015, 10:00 am - 12:00 pm

Location: Ma On Shan Park, Hong Kong (Map)

Event Details

We hope you'll join us at one of our favorite parks in Hong Kong for a fun picnic! Just take the MTR to Ma On Shan and then walk through Ma On Shan Plaza. The park is on the other side. There are two playgrounds, we can meet by the first one if you go to the left once you arrive at the park. Please bring some snacks and drinks, and picnic mats!

If you have any questions, don't hesitate to call me. See you soon!
